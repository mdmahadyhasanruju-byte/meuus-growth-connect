# meUus Soul Source Inventory v1.0

## Purpose

This inventory records the current tracked meUus Soul source and content evidence,
along with the boundaries that apply before rebuilding or expanding meUus Soul.
It is a preservation and planning record, not approval to import older material,
expand public routes, or change public claims.

## Current verified repo evidence

The following evidence is verified in the tracked repository at the time of this
inventory:

- `src/routes/soul.tsx` provides the existing `/soul` gateway. It describes
  meUus Soul as a developing public knowledge and reflection gateway, identifies
  current public starting points, and states that it is not a completed library.
- `src/routes/book.tsx` provides the existing `/book` gateway. It presents Book
  meUus as a developing writing and reflection project, not a finalized or
  released book.
- `src/routes/knowledge-before-action.tsx` provides the existing

  automated analysis, or a complete DLAS system.
- `src/data/navigation.ts` includes meUus Soul and Book meUus under the
  `Knowledge & Reflection` Explorer group. Their statuses are `In development`
  and `Book project in development`.
- `docs/source-of-truth/meuus-total-work-inventory-v001.md` records that older
  Soul-focused sites, book documents, learning structures, DLAS presentations,

  requires editorial, provenance, legal, privacy, implementation, rights, and

No older Soul archive or external content is imported or approved by this
inventory.

## What meUus Soul is intended to become

### Direction — not complete production state

The intended direction is:

- a deeper learning hub;
- reflection and knowledge paths;
- a writing and book direction;
- a future possibility for English and Bangla content;
- examples, explanations, questions and answers, a glossary, exercises, and
  related learning paths; and
- a bridge from the public meUus foundation to deeper learning.

These points describe direction only. They do not establish that the full hub,
content library, bilingual experience, editorial system, or review process is
complete or live.

## Current boundaries

- meUus Soul is not yet fully rebuilt.
- Old or archived material must be inventoried and reviewed before reuse.
- Existing or recovered content is not automatically final, complete,
  professionally reviewed, or legally, medically, or religiously authoritative.
- meUus Soul is not a substitute for emergency, medical, legal,
  religious-authority, psychological, employment, or financial support.
- A tracked gateway or planning record does not prove that a deeper learning
  feature, content library, account system, AI service, or DLAS runtime is live.

## Preservation rules

- Preserve original source material before editing it.
- Record each source location, its status, the reason for any proposed reuse,
  and any claim corrections required.
- Do not delete old content until it has been inventoried.
- Do not silently rewrite the founder's meaning; document material editorial or
  interpretive changes.
- Do not import large archives directly into public routes.
- Keep archive evidence separate from approved production content.

## Claim correction rules

- Distinguish vision from live reality.
- Distinguish learning and reflection from professional or personal advice.
- Distinguish founder writing from professional review.
- Remove, qualify, or soften claims that exceed available evidence.
- Label unfinished, draft, in-development, and archive material clearly.
- Recheck claims when moving material between internal evidence, editorial
  review, and public use.

## Content status categories

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| `Verified live` | Confirmed in the current public production surface and described within its verified limits. |
| `Tracked gateway` | A tracked public entry page that points toward developing work without representing a complete destination. |
| `Draft/source material` | Working material that may inform future content but is not approved public truth. |
| `Archive evidence` | Preserved historical material that proves earlier work existed, not that it is current or approved. |
| `Needs claim review` | Material whose wording, evidence, status, or promises must be corrected before public use. |
| `Needs professional review` | Material requiring qualified review because of legal, medical, psychological, religious, financial, safeguarding, or other high-stakes implications. |
| `Needs translation review` | English, Bangla, or bilingual material requiring meaning, terminology, accessibility, and cultural review. |
| `Do not publish yet` | Material that must remain non-public until its source, rights, safety, claims, and intended use are resolved. |
