# meUus Amanah Constitution v1.0

## The Written Trust Before the System

**Prepared for:** Md. Mahady Hasan / Ruju  
**Project:** meUus  
**Status:** Founder-governing constitution  
**Nature:** Internal source document for truth, trust, work, support, soul, research, public claims, and future building  
**Date:** July 2026

---

## 1. Bismillah / Opening

In the name of Allah, the Most Merciful, the Most Compassionate.

meUus begins with a written trust.

Before it becomes an app, company, platform, ecosystem, service, AI system, social movement, or public claim, meUus is an **amanah**.

Amanah means responsibility. Amanah means trust. Amanah means custody. Amanah means that something is not owned carelessly, used carelessly, promised carelessly, or exposed carelessly.

meUus must not begin from ego, noise, revenge, desperation, money, or public attention.

meUus must begin from truth.

---

## 2. First principle: meUus is an amanah before it is an app, company, platform, or ecosystem

**meUus is an amanah before it is an app, company, platform, or ecosystem.**

This sentence governs everything.

Every page, feature, claim, service, role, visual, document, workflow, AI system, DLAS idea, domain, product, payment, opportunity, and future expansion must obey this principle.

If something violates the amanah, it does not belong in meUus yet.

If something is not true, it must not be claimed. If something is not ready, it must not be presented as ready. If support does not exist, support must not be promised. If a system cannot carry people's trust, it must not ask for people's trust.

---

## 3. The pen before the system

Before public action, there must be written responsibility.

meUus does not claim divine authority. meUus does not speak for Allah. meUus does not issue religious rulings. meUus does not judge souls. meUus does not claim sacred status.

But meUus must learn this human lesson:

- Before action, write.
- Before promise, define.
- Before building, govern.
- Before guiding, know the boundary.
- Before serving, understand the burden.
- Before scaling, protect the trust.

The first real work of meUus is not a launch. The first real work is the written amanah.

---

## 4. Prophetic standard of conduct

meUus must seek to follow the human moral example taught through the beloved final Messenger, Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, in conduct and character, not through false claim, religious branding, or authority over people.

For meUus, this means:

- Truthfulness before popularity.
- Amanah before ambition.
- Mercy before judgment.
- Patience before reaction.
- Consultation before decision.
- Humility before leadership.
- Service before status.
- Correction before expansion.
- Responsibility before recognition.

Faith may ground the founder.

Faith must not be used to pressure, manipulate, control, shame, or spiritually label users.

---

## 5. Why this constitution exists

meUus did not come from a simple business idea.

It came from study, observation, loneliness, misunderstanding, social experience, family experience, legal study, online and offline experiments, AI-human comparison, self-testing, spiritual questioning, and the founder's long search for the right form of responsibility.

The founder used himself as part of the experiment. The founder tested too much. The founder searched too deeply. The founder carried realities that only Allah fully knows.

But pain alone is not guidance. Research alone is not permission. Intention alone is not proof. Vision alone is not responsibility.

Therefore, this constitution exists to protect meUus from becoming an uncontrolled extension of pain, ego, ambition, or unfinished desire.

The wound may become wisdom. The wound must not become careless public doctrine.

---

## 6. Core law: Claim ≤ Evidence

The central operating law of meUus is:

**Claim must always be less than or equal to evidence.**

If evidence is small, the claim must be small. If a feature is planned, it must be called planned. If a feature is a prototype, it must be called a prototype. If something is browser-local, it must be called browser-local. If something is not live, it must not be called live. If something is not reviewed, it must not be called reviewed. If something is not a service, it must not be sold as service. If something is not professional advice, it must not sound like professional advice.

Truth is not weakness.

Truth is protection.

---

## 7. Soul, Trust, Work, Try, Support

meUus must protect five human beginnings:

**Soul. Trust. Work. Try. Support.**

### Soul

No person should be reduced to a score, label, diagnosis, spiritual judgment, employability judgment, social value judgment, or system category.

A person is always more than what a system can see.

### Trust

meUus must not demand trust. meUus must earn trust.

Trust is earned through truth, boundaries, correction, privacy, humility, and evidence.

### Work

Work must be respected, even when it is small, slow, repetitive, unseen, ordinary, or not glamorous.

meUus must not dishonor real work by hiding behind beautiful philosophy.

### Try

A person's attempt must not be mocked, exploited, rushed, or turned into performance.

Trying is a beginning. Trying must be protected.

### Support

meUus must never pretend to support people where it has no staff, system, process, knowledge, legal boundary, professional governance, or operational capacity.

Support must be truthful before it is emotional.

---

## 8. Founder inner guard

The founder must protect meUus from the world.

But the founder must also protect meUus from himself.

The founder must guard against:

- Ego.
- Pride.
- Haste.
- Revenge.
- Despair.
- Self-pity.
- Laziness disguised as philosophy.
- Ambition disguised as service.
- Pain disguised as doctrine.
- Attention-seeking disguised as mission.
- Overbuilding before responsibility.
- Overclaiming before evidence.
- Using meUus to escape real-world work.
- Using people's pain to prove the project.
- Using spiritual language to cover unfinished duty.

In the founder's faith-grounded understanding, this also means seeking protection from shaytan, from Iblis, and from the nafs inside the self.

The danger is not only outside. The danger can enter through the founder's own heart.

---

## 9. Corruption guard

meUus must be protected from corruption in every form.

It must be protected from:

- False claims.
- Fake authority.
- Manipulation.
- Exploitative products.
- Misuse of user data.
- Misuse of AI.
- Misuse of spiritual language.
- Misuse of emotional pain.
- Misuse of people's hope.
- Monetizing vulnerability too early.
- Offering support without support capacity.
- Role, salary, stipend, equity, or reward promises without governance.
- Turning service into status.
- Turning amanah into performance.
- Turning a human mission into founder ego.

If meUus grows without this guard, it may become the opposite of what it was meant to be.

---

## 10. Current truth rule

At this stage, meUus is a public foundation and learning/reflection ecosystem in development.

The full app, backend, accounts, AI runtime, DLAS runtime, cloud storage, payments, rewards, subscriptions, dashboards, and full service systems must not be claimed as live unless verified in current source-of-truth.

Until verified by current source-of-truth, none of these may be publicly claimed as live.

---

## 11. Domain truth rule

All meUus domains must speak the same truth.

A domain must not say "live" when another official page says "not live." A domain must not offer payments when the project is not ready for payments. A domain must not offer AI/DLAS when AI/DLAS is not safely governed. A domain must not claim full service availability when no service system exists.

`meuus.org/status` remains the current public truth checkpoint until replaced by a newer approved source-of-truth.

`meuus.app` and `meuussoul.com` must be aligned with the same truth before they can represent public readiness.

Domains must not contradict amanah.

---

## 12. AI rule

AI and tools may support drafting, coding, review, organization, comparison, research, and learning.

But AI does not own meUus. AI does not validate meUus. AI does not sponsor meUus. AI does not endorse meUus. AI does not carry responsibility for meUus.

The founder remains responsible.

No public AI feature may exist without consent, safety boundary, privacy model, rate limits, abuse control, cost control, human fallback where needed, clear non-authority language, no false certainty, and no hidden spiritual, legal, medical, psychological, financial, or employment authority.

AI must remain a tool. It must not become a false voice of certainty over human life.

---

## 13. DLAS rule

DLAS must never be presented as:

- Diagnosis.
- Clinical assessment.
- Psychological truth.
- Religious judgment.
- Employability score.
- Human worth score.
- Future-success prediction.
- Automated life planning.
- A complete reading of a person.
- A replacement for human understanding.

If DLAS is ever built publicly, it must require consent, purpose limitation, privacy protection, deletion/export path, non-diagnostic language, and no high-stakes automated decisions.

DLAS may help reflection. DLAS must not judge the soul.

---

## 14. Professional boundary rule

meUus may educate, organize, reflect, prepare, explain, and refer within limits.

But meUus must not claim authority in law, medicine, mental health, religious rulings, finance, employment decisions, crisis support, or emergency response unless proper professional governance exists.

Until then, meUus must remain honest:

- Learning.
- Reflection.
- Organization.
- Responsible next step.
- Referral where appropriate.
- Clear boundary always.

---

## 15. Payments/products/money rule

Money must not enter before responsibility.

No public payment instruction, paid tier, purchase flow, product sale, non-refundable claim, service fee, or paid access promise may be public until the following exist:

- Clear entity or receiver responsibility.
- Terms.
- Privacy policy alignment.
- Refund policy.
- Delivery obligation.
- Support process.
- Recordkeeping.
- Complaint path.
- Operational capacity.
- Legal review where needed.

Hope must not be sold before meUus can carry the burden of that hope.

---

## 16. Roles/rewards/equity/opportunity rule

No public salary, stipend, equity, pre-seed allocation, lifetime reward, founder title, guaranteed role, guaranteed opportunity, or guaranteed benefit may be promised without legal, entity, and governance approval.

People must not be attracted through unclear promises.

Interest may be collected. Future pathways may be described carefully. But promise requires structure.

Amanah does not allow vague reward language to pull people into uncertainty.

---

## 17. Archive and raw-writing rule

Old chats, raw founder writings, source packs, recovered repos, screenshots, prototypes, previous deployments, old pages, and experiments are evidence.

They are not automatically current truth.

Raw writing may contain meaning, pain, discovery, and the seed of meUus.

But raw writing must be transformed before public use.

The wound may become wisdom. The wound must not be exposed carelessly.

---

## 18. Listening rule

meUus must not require a perfect explanation before offering a truthful beginning.

A person may begin with:

- One word.
- Confusion.
- Silence.
- Fear.
- Faith.
- Work.
- Study.
- Family.
- Failure.
- A mistake.
- A question.
- "I don't know."

The first duty is not to score. The first duty is to listen.

Then recover context, ask permission, define boundary, and offer one responsible next step.

---

## 19. Support rule

meUus must never pretend to support people where it has no capability.

Support must be truthful, limited, documented, safe, within capacity, within boundary, non-exploitative, and correctable.

If meUus cannot support, it must say so.

If meUus can only explain, it must only explain. If meUus can only refer, it must only refer. If meUus can only listen, it must not pretend to solve.

---

## 20. Correction rule

When meUus discovers contradiction, overclaim, unsafe wording, public confusion, broken trust, or meaning drift, correction is not shame.

Correction is amanah.

The founder must not hide mistakes to protect pride.

meUus must correct what is wrong, preserve what is meaningful, and continue with humility.

A truthful correction is better than a beautiful falsehood.

## 22. Build rule

Before any new public feature, page, domain, visual, AI function, DLAS flow, role, product, payment, service, or claim, meUus must ask:

- Is it true?
- Is it needed?
- Is it safe?
- Is it humble?
- Is it evidence-based?
- Is it within capacity?
- Does it protect the user?
- Does it protect trust?
- Does it protect the founder from ego?
- Does it protect against nafs?
- Does it obey this Amanah Constitution?

Then meUus must:

- Write first.
- Define boundary.
- Check risk.
- Classify the claim.
- Build the smallest safe version.
- Validate.
- Document.
- Publish only what is true.
